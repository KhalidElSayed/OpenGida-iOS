OpenGida-iOS

Mobile iOS client for the Open Gida Basque Government's oGov platform

As part of the Open Irekia project, the Basque Government has released under the EUPL bits of free software. This is an unofficial mirror of the software releases for the Open Gida iOS mobile client. See the github repo. Or download the app for iOS.
